Product Thinking Vocabulary

  • Painkiller
    • A pain killer reduces costs in a business model inorder to increase revenue.
  • Adopters
    • There are 4 types of consumers: innovators, pragmatists, conservatives, and skeptics. These represent generally the stages at which technology is adopted by certain groups with innovators being the first to adopt and skpetics being the last and most difficult to adopt.
  • Value Proposition
    • A provable and repeatable promise of value to customers about why they should adopt your product as opposed to a competitor's product.
